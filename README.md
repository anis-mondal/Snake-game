# 🐍 Ultimate Serpentine - Premium Snake Game

Welcome to **Ultimate Serpentine**, a highly modernized, feature-rich take on the classic Snake game! Experience buttery-smooth gameplay, dynamic RGB themes, unlimited combos, and satisfying haptic feedback right in your browser.

[![Play Now - Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/PLAY_NOW-Live_Demo-3B82F6?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://anis-mondal.github.io/Snake-game/)

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **🎮 Advanced Controls:** Play seamlessly using the modernized, beautifully designed on-screen D-Pad, or simply swipe anywhere on the screen for intuitive gesture controls.
* **⚡ Unlimited Combo System:** Eat food consecutively within the time limit to build an infinite combo multiplier! Your score and special coin drops multiply exponentially the faster you eat.
* **📈 Dynamic Leveling & Economy:** * Earn XP by eating food and building combos.
  * Level up to earn escalating Coin rewards (Level 2 gives 50🪙, Level 3 gives 60🪙, etc.).
  * Collect special "Coin Drops" that scale with your current combo multiplier.
* **🛟 Revive System:** Made a mistake? Use 20 coins to instantly revive your snake safely at the center of the screen and keep your massive score going!
* **📱 Premium Haptics & Sound:** Enjoy a fully immersive experience with distinct sound effects and powerful device vibrations (haptics) for moving, eating, leveling up, and game overs.
* **📺 Fullscreen Support:** A dedicated toggle to instantly switch to a distraction-free fullscreen mode.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

This project is built using modern web technologies:
* **HTML5 Canvas:** For high-resolution, buttery-smooth 2D rendering.
* **Tailwind CSS:** For sleek, responsive, and glassmorphism-styled UI components.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** Game logic, economy storage, and animation loops.
* **Web Audio API & Vibration API:** For zero-latency sounds and haptic feedback.

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Click the [**Play Now**](https://anis-mondal.github.io/Snake-game/) button above to launch the game.
2. Use the **On-Screen D-Pad** or **Swipe** on your touch screen to steer the snake.
3. Eat the glowing orbs to gain XP, Score, and build your Combo Multiplier.
4. Catch the golden **"C"** coins to earn in-game currency.
5. Use your coins in the **Options (Menu)** to unlock new Skins, Themes, and AMOLED mode!

---

> **Note:** Haptic feedback requires a supported mobile device. High refresh rate displays will experience incredibly smooth frame pacing due to the custom `requestAnimationFrame` loop.
